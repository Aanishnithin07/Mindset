Fixed Mindset or Growth Mindset?

Carol Dweck's research found that people tend to relate to difficulty, failure, effort, feedback, and other people's success in one of two ways — as threats to defend against, or as material to work with. Most of us are a mix, and the mix changes by area of life. Answer honestly, not how you wish you'd answer, and see where you actually land.
